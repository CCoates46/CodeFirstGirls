Meet The Team

Ben Coates

Co-Founder and Director

Heading up Cande’s video offering, Ben has worked in production for
20 years. He started his career in television developing show formats
and producing content for UK broadcasters including the BBC and ITV.
Since migrating into branded video production he has led in-house and
freelance production teams to deliver content for broadcast, web, social
and video-on-demand services including Sky, C4 and ITV.

At Cande Group Ben works with clients in the early stages, shaping ideas
and developing content strategies with them. He oversees all production and
livestream delivery and is an inaugural member of the University of
Wolverhampton’s Media Advisory Board.

Phil Coates

LiveStream Producer

LA-based Phil brings a wealth of streaming experience to Cande. Working
with Push.Live he was part of the team that streamed Virgin Galactic’s maiden
voyage to the edge of space from Spaceport America in New Mexico and he has
streamed live music events for headline acts across America and Europe.

Phil assists Cande’s livestream offering and helps us to deliver
location-based streams from anywhere, often remote vision mixing content
being shot by our team in the UK.
